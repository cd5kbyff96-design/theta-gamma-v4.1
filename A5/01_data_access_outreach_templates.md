## Template 1: Initial Data Access Request

**Use when:** First contact with a data provider to request access to a new dataset.

---

**Subject:** Data Access Request — [DATASET_NAME] for Cross-Modal Research

Dear [CONTACT_NAME],

I am writing to request access to [DATASET_NAME] for use in the Theta-Gamma cross-modal research project at [ORGANIZATION].

**Project Summary:** Theta-Gamma is developing a cross-modal model that integrates [text/image/audio] modalities. We are currently in the [baseline/mid-tier/pilot] phase and require [DATASET_NAME] to [specific purpose — e.g., "train cross-modal alignment on paired text-image data" or "evaluate retrieval performance on a standard benchmark"].

**Intended Use:**
- Training / Evaluation / Both (select one)
- Modalities needed: [text / image / audio / paired combinations]
- Estimated data volume: [N samples / X GB]
- Duration of access: [X months / perpetual for research]
- Environment: [on-premises / cloud (specify provider) / hybrid]

**Data Handling Commitments:**
- Data will be stored in [encrypted cloud storage / on-premises servers] with access restricted to [N] authorized team members.
- Data will not be redistributed, sublicensed, or shared with third parties.
- We will comply with all terms of your data use agreement.
- Data will be deleted upon project completion or license expiration, whichever comes first.
- We can provide our institutional data protection policies upon request.

**Security & Compliance:**
- Our infrastructure meets [SOC 2 / ISO 27001 / institutional security policy] standards.
- PII handling: [We will / will not] process personally identifiable information. If PII is present, we will apply [anonymization / pseudonymization] before use.
- IRB/Ethics review: [Completed — approval #XXX / In progress / Not required for this data type].

**Timeline:** We would appreciate access by [DATE] to meet our [GATE — e.g., "G2 mid-tier evaluation"] milestone. However, we are flexible and can adjust our timeline to accommodate your review process.

**Attachments:**
- [ ] Project overview (1-page summary)
- [ ] Data use agreement (if your organization requires one from us)
- [ ] Institutional data protection policy
- [ ] IRB/Ethics approval letter (if applicable)

I am happy to schedule a call to discuss our project in more detail or address any questions about our data handling practices.

Thank you for your consideration.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
[YOUR_TITLE]
[YOUR_ORGANIZATION]
[YOUR_EMAIL] | [YOUR_PHONE]

## Template 4: Multi-Source Data Partnership Proposal

**Use when:** Requesting an ongoing data partnership rather than one-time access.

---

Dear [CONTACT_NAME],

We are exploring a research data partnership with [PROVIDER_ORGANIZATION] to support the Theta-Gamma cross-modal model project. Rather than a one-time data access request, we are interested in an ongoing collaboration that benefits both parties.

**What We Are Proposing:**
- Access to [DATASET(S)] for training and evaluation over [DURATION]
- Periodic data refreshes ([monthly / quarterly]) to maintain data currency
- Shared benchmarking results on your data to demonstrate cross-modal capabilities

**What We Can Offer:**
- [Co-authorship on publications using your data]
- [Benchmark results and model performance reports on your datasets]
- [Access to our evaluation tools and metrics framework]
- [Acknowledgment in all publications and presentations]
- [Other specific value proposition]

**Proposed Structure:**
- Phase 1 ([MONTHS 1-3]): Initial data access, baseline evaluation
- Phase 2 ([MONTHS 4-6]): Full training integration, mid-tier results
- Phase 3 ([MONTHS 7-12]): Pilot results, joint benchmarking, publication

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss this proposal in a 30-minute call. Please let me know your availability in the next two weeks.

Best regards,
[YOUR_NAME]
